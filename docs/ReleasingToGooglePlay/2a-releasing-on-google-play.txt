releasing on google play
see: https://developer.android.com/studio/publish
     https://developer.android.com/distribute/google-play

need a google play developer account

the process

  i.   prepare promotional materials
         - provide screenshots
         - videos 
         - graphics
         - promotional text

  ii.  configure options
         - options include country, language, price
         - choose details such as application type, category, content rating
  
  iii. upload assets
         - after options are configured, promotional materials can be uploaded
         - application can also be uploaded as a draft (unpublished)

  iv. publish application's release version
        - click publish in the play console
